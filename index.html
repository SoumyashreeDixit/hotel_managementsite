Overview

The Department of Tourism and Hospitality at CV Raman Global University, Bhubaneswar, Odisha offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs to students interested in pursuing careers in the tourism and hospitality industry. The department aims to provide its students with a strong foundation in both theoretical and practical aspects of the industry, preparing them for a successful career in this dynamic field. The faculty comprises experienced industry professionals and academic experts who bring their knowledge and expertise to the classroom, providing students with a real-world understanding of the industry. The curriculum is designed to cover a broad range of topics, including hospitality management, tourism marketing, food and beverage management, event management, sustainable tourism, and cultural heritage tourism. The department is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including a food production lab, a training restaurant, and a front office lab, providing students with hands-on training and practical experience in the field. The department also offers opportunities for internships and industry collaborations, allowing students to gain practical experience and network with industry professionals. Overall, the Department of Tourism and Hospitality at CV Raman Global University is committed to providing its students with a comprehensive education that prepares them for a successful career in tourism and hospitality industry.

Objective

The objective of the Department of Tourism and Hospitality at CV Raman Global University is to provide a comprehensive education to students aspiring to pursue careers in the hospitality and tourism industry. The department aims to equip students with a deep understanding of the principles, concepts, and practices of the industry, and to develop their skills in areas such as customer service, communication, leadership, problem-solving, and critical thinking. The department aims to provide a dynamic and stimulating learning environment that encourages students to think creatively and to develop innovative solutions to the challenges facing the industry. The curriculum is designed to be practical and industry-focused, providing students with hands-on experience through internships, field trips, and other experiential learning opportunities. The department also aims to develop students’ awareness of the social, cultural, and environmental impacts of tourism and hospitality, and to promote sustainable and responsible practices in the industry. Through a range of courses and initiatives, the department seeks to prepare students to become responsible leaders and agents of change in the industry, capable of contributing to the growth and development of the sector while preserving its natural and cultural resources for future generations.
