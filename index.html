Students not only from India but also from other countries are enrolled in the hospitality faculty. The facilitators here have decade long experience in teaching, which help bring in holistic all-round development of the young minds. Programmes Overview at

CGU,BBSR

BHMCT(4 years)

A unique programme integrating the artistic and scientific aspects of food preparation, designed by top chefs in India.

  • Provides training in advanced cooking techniques for Indian and global cuisines.
  • Specialisations in Food Science & Innovation, Culinary Nutrition & Wellness and Restaurant Operation Management.
  • Covers culinary nutrition, focusing on balancing taste, nutrition, and market trends.
  • Emphasises food processing technology, food microbiology, and culinary innovation.
  • Includes modules on restaurant operation management, event planning, and food media.
  • Prepares students for careers in food styling, culinary entrepreneurship, and catering.
  • Hands-on learning with state-of-the-art kitchens and real-world restaurant operations.

BSC in Hotel & Hotel Administration(3 years)

A four-year programme designed to build a strong foundation in hospitality and restaurant operations, management and entrepreneurship.

  • Covers core areas including food production, food & beverage service, front office, accommodation operations and management studies.
  • Focuses on developing leadership skills, professionalism and contextual knowledge tailored to the hospitality industry's needs.
  • Includes internships with leading hospitality brands to provide hands-on, real-world experience.
  • Emphasises health, safety, cultural, societal, legal and environmental considerations in hospitality practices.
  • Focuses on strategy, customer service, operations management, and hospitality marketing to prepare students for leadership roles.

WHY CGU

Modern Facilities

  • Advanced Training Kitchens & Bakeries: Contemporary learning spaces with hands-on culinary training.
  • Industry-Standard Hospitality Software: Training on Opera, IDS PMS and CESIM software.
  • Dedicated Student Training Spaces: Restaurants, Front Office, Housekeeping Labs and Guest Rooms.
  • Student-Run Food Pod: Practical exposure to operations and service delivery.

Vibrant Clubs and Organisations

  • Student-Run Ventures:On-campus initiatives such as Classic Café, Ministry of Bakery (MOB) and a Gourmet Store provide real-world business and operational experience.
  • Creative & Professional Clubs:Gastronomy Club, Wine & Spirits Club, Cultural Club, On the Rocks Club, Accommodation Club, Digital & Social Media Club, Sports Club, Literary Club, Quiz Club and WGSHA Horticulture (Green House/Spice Garden), enabling holistic development beyond classrooms.

Dynamic Events at CGU

  • Culinary festivals, hospitality fairs, cultural showcases, regular simulations, Culinary Boot Camps, Wine Appreciation Programmes and Food Festivals.
  • These platforms encourage collaboration, innovation, industry interaction and practical exposure in live environments.

Extensive Alumni Engagement

  • A strong alumni network actively supports students through endowment awards, workshops, seminars, guest lectures and mentorship.
  • Alumni involvement also plays a key role in strengthening industry connections and placement opportunities.

Certifications

  • Certification programmes include Wine Certification by WSET (UK) and courses from City & Guilds (UK).
  • CGU-Odisha is the only college to offer these certifications as an integrated part of the academic curriculum.

Online Resources and Platforms

  • Access to leading academic journals and databases such as Scopus, Web of Science, Scival, Sage, Springer, Taylor & Francis, John Wiley, EBSCO (Hospitality & Tourism Complete) and CAC+HTLC.
  • Supported by LMS and SLCM platforms for seamless academic delivery and learning management.
